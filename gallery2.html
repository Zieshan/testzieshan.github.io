For the love of Nature

Italian Vi

Nature. A place where busy individuals find rest and comfort. The following photos are shot to simulate the euforic feeling of the adventurous. As cities grow, fewer people get the chance to experience nature`s beauty. With this series, Zieshan tries to remind the urbanized civilization of this beauty, but also brings the outside closer to peoples homes. All pictures are shot in Italy, a country known for its pizza, coffee and from now on nature.
